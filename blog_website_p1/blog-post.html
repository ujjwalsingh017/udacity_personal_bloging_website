I love wandering through Indian Monuments. When asked about my experience, I can only respond:

India is the country of my dreams: Modernity and tradition are fusing together and constantly give birth to new creations of light and shadow. The diwali lights are flickerung and bathing the city in its signature glow and giving me life.

Now studying at Galgotias University I spents my days at the university, but the night is when I truly come alive.

I hope that more and more people will see the true beauty of India soon. Explore the city in your own pace and discover things you have never seen before.
